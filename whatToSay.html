What Should You Say!


Talking is your strong suit!


"How many qualifying payments do I have?"

"At this time, I do not want to give you an estimation because I don't want to be too high or too low from the actual total. But good news is that once the application has been approved, our PSLF agent John will reach out to you with the confirmed amount. Best part is that he will actually have an estimated date of completion too."

This is the quickest way to get a member to not just drop the question but the fastest way to get the docs back from them. When I talk to a member and use this line, I see them in my apply queue within the week.

"Is there an update on my application?"

Hint: Log into FL or servicers if notes are older than 5 days.

  1. With Fedloan. "At this time there is no update on you FedLoan account. John reviewed and submitted the application on X/XX/XXXX and normally it takes 4 to 8 weeks for the docs to be approved. As soon as they are though, John will reach out to you Via email and call to go over everything.
  2. With Fedloan. "Yes! It looks like it was approved recently. I am sure you're in John's queue to reach out to today or tomorrow but since you called I'll send him an email to do your case next and he will each out as soon as he is done reviewing everything." -Send a task to me and I'll get on it ASAP AKA end of day.
  3. Other Servicer. "Looks like your loans are still with your current servicer. There is no information on the loan transfer to Fedloan at this current time. As soon as there is an update, John will reach out to you with the next steps in the process. From the submittion date it can take 2 to 3 months for the loans to be fully transferred."
  4. Other Servicer. "Ohh, looks like there was an update the other day. Per a letter in your inbox your loans should transfer to FedLoan X/XX/XXXX. Once the transfer starts John will be reaching out to you to create a FedLoan Login and to make sure transfer us complete with no issues."

"I don't think my payments are correct"

  1. PSLF 4 closed: "Our PSLF manager has reviewed all payments and comfirmed they are accurat." Then explain, dates and payment amounts noted in case notes.
  2. Working PSLF 4 docs post: "Your documents posted recently John with reach out to you soon with the actual count and if there is any issue he will schedule a conference call with you." - Send task docs post
  3. PSLF 4 conference call request: "Yes, John agrees with you and in his notes he would like to do a conference  call to fix the issue. When do you have free time?" - Come to me and see if I'm free if not I will give you times I am.
  4. PSLF 3 or before: "Unfortunately, we need to complete the/a new application first. Once it is complete our PSLF Specialist will review all the approved docs and make sure everything is accurate. If they are, he will let you know and if there are any discrepencies he will let you know and schedule a conference  call with FedLoan to fix the issue."

    5. This is basically the basics for ever basic question a member would ask. Always feel free to come up to me with a question you have or a member has.
